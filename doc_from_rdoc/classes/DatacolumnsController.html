update_datatype()

This method is called whenever someone clicks on the ‘Save Data Type’ Button in the Data Column approval process.

The datatype of this Data Column is saved and the respective Sheetcells are updated.

update_invalid_values()

creates categories for all invalid values completed in the form and assigns the category to the sheetcell

update_metadata()

The meta data of this Data Column is saved. The people submitted via form are assigned to the Data Column or their assignation is revoked.
